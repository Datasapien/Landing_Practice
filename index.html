Welcome to 36 Livingstone

Here at 36 Livingstone Road, we pride ourselves on our ability to provide a nurturing and supportive environment for your son or daughter to thrive in. In addition to teaching the traditional 'adulting' responsibilities such as bill paying, cleaning up after yourself and ordering takeaway during a hangover, we also offer a range of extra-curricular options to mould your son or daughter into an interesting member of society. These include, but are not limited to: boardgaming, gardening, Netflix binging, house plant killing and tea bag sharing.
